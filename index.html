Unfinished Tasks

  1. Decide Tools to use

    Sigma or Google Doc or PDF?
    Sigma: Not compatible with screenreader users.
    Google Doc: Easy to use and free. Good for group work.
    PDF: For final version, our deliverable. But google doc document provided as well for the Dev team to edit if necessary.

    Comments:
    Researcher: Since we need to let The Office for Students with Disabilities (OSD) test our guidebook and give feedback, better to use Google Doc, but PDF is good for readability.

    PM is responsible for the overall design of the guidebook, including the cover and the format.
